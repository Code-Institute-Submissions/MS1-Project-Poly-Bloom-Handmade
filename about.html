About:

Poly Bloom started in early 2020 but has long since been a dream of mine. I have always loved earrings. I love to collect them when travelling abroad. Seeing what locals in different countries are designing has always inspired me.

I became curious about making my own jewelery and began to learn about polymer clay designs in earrings. I made my first batch in February and I have not been able to stop ever since.
